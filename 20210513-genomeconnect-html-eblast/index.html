Engage Your Patients in Data Sharing!

Individuals who have had genetic testing can provide valuable information to help inform our understanding of genetics and health, benefiting individual patients and the broader genetics community.

 

Individuals are an important source of genetic information for public databases such as ClinVar

Individuals sharing data can provide more details about their health history

Individuals can remain informed about their genetic results

Individuals can connect with other other participants, clinicians, and researchers
 

 ClinGen partners with individual patients through its own registry (GenomeConnect) as well as with other gene or condition specific registries to support individuals interested in data sharing.

Learn more about how you can engage patients in data sharing:

If you interact with patients, tell them about the opportunity to enroll in GenomeConnect.
If you work with a patient registry, discuss the opportunity to work with ClinGen to share data.
